Creating a Awareness For Food Management

Food management is the process of planning, organizing, and controlling food resources to ensure efficiency, quality, and sustainability. It involves proper storage, preparation, and distribution of food while minimizing waste and ensuring nutritional value. Effective food management is essential in households, restaurants, and large-scale industries to maintain food safety, reduce costs, and meet dietary needs. This practice also plays a crucial role in addressing global food security by promoting sustainable sourcing, reducing food loss, and encouraging responsible consumption. By implementing smart strategies such as portion control, inventory tracking, and meal planning, food management helps create a balanced and efficient food system.
